About us

Safari Freight is a proudly Kenyan logistics and transportation company .

that delivers reliable, timely, and secure freight solutions across East Africa. Founded with a mission to simplify cargo movement in the region, we specialize in ground transport, warehousing, packaging, and full logistics services tailored for businesses of all sizes. Whether it’s rural last-mile delivery or urban bulk movement, our team ensures every shipment is handled with professionalism and care. At Safari Freight, we believe in building trust through transparency, efficiency, and local knowledge. Our name – Safari – means “journey,” and we walk every step of it with you and your cargo.

Our Services

GROUND TRANSPORT

We offer efficient and timely ground transport services across Kenya and East Africa. Our fleet is equipped to handle various cargo sizes safely and quickly.

WAREHOUSING

Safari Freight offers secure, spacious, and well-managed warehouses to keep your goods safe and organized. Our facilities are ideal for both short-term and long-term storage needs.

PACKAGING AND STORING

We provide careful packaging solutions and safe storage for goods of all types. Whether fragile, heavy, or bulky, your cargo is professionally packed and stored until it’s ready for transport.

LOGISTICS SERVICES

From pickup to delivery, we manage every step of the logistics process. Safari Freight ensures seamless coordination, real-time updates, and dependable service for all your supply chain needs.
