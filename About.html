About us

Safari Freight is a proudly Kenyan logistics and transportation company .

About Safari-Freight Our Brand Story Safari-Freight was born from a passion for making logistics simpler and more reliable across Africa. What started as a small idea to help local businesses move goods more efficiently has now grown into a trusted name in regional and international freight services. We believe logistics should empower growth, not slow it down — and that's exactly what we set out to do. Who We Serve We proudly serve a wide range of clients — from small business owners and e-commerce startups to large manufacturers and import/export companies. Whether you're moving one package or an entire shipment, we’re here to deliver. How We Operate Safari-Freight combines technology with human service. We track every shipment in real time, communicate transparently, and partner with trusted carriers to ensure your cargo arrives safely, affordably, and on time. Our system is optimized for both road and sea freight, with planned expansion into air logistics. The Face of Our Business Behind Safari-Freight is a passionate team of logistics experts, software developers, and customer care professionals. Our founder, Roy Murimi, began the journey after seeing the logistical challenges local businesses faced firsthand — and built Safari-Freight to change that. What Makes Us Different Our platform is built with our clients in mind. We offer: Fast online bookings Real-time tracking Transparent pricing Personalized support We’re not just moving cargo — we’re moving businesses forward. Our Business Model We operate on a digital-first freight management system that connects shippers with verified carriers. Our income model includes service fees, optional insurance, and custom freight solutions tailored to your business needs. Recognition & Trust Safari-Freight has been featured in regional logistics forums, praised for innovation in East African supply chain solutions. We've received excellent reviews from early users and continue to grow based on strong word-of-mouth and reliability.
